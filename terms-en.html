2 Service Description

AR is an iOS SaaS platform enabling businesses to digitize their products into 3D/AR format using the iPhone camera. Core features include:

  • Photogrammetry-based 3D modeling using the iPhone camera (Object Capture)
  • On-device storage of created models (all plans)
  • Exporting 3D models in USDZ and OBJ formats (Plans 1, 2, and 3)
  • Cloud uploading models to Cloudflare R2 (Plan 3 only)
  • QR code and AR embed code generation (Plan 3 only)
  • Web embed codes for AR viewing on external websites (Plan 3 only)
Technical Requirement: The Object Capture feature requires an iPhone 12 or newer running iOS 17.0+. Devices that do not meet these requirements cannot run the AR scanning feature.

6 Cancellation & Data Deletion

Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription via iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, or via the in-app link. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Access rights after cancellation apply as follows:

  • Immediately: Your account enters a "grace period".
  • Day 7: QR links, AR embed codes, and cloud access are revoked. In-app models become locked.
  • Day 30: All model files stored in Cloudflare R2 are permanently deleted.
⚠️ Warning: Cloud models deleted after the 30-day period cannot be recovered. We recommend using the in-app export feature to back up your models.

Account Deletion

You may permanently delete your account at any time directly from within the app:

  • Open the app → Settings tab → "Delete Account Permanently"
  • Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
  • All your data (models, cloud files, profile) will be permanently and irreversibly deleted.
⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. All cloud models will be deleted from Cloudflare R2 immediately. Local models on your device are not affected.

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. Subscription billing must be managed separately in Apple subscription settings.

Plan 1 / Plan 2 Cancellation

Because models in these plans are stored only on your device, they remain on your device after cancellation. You will not be able to perform new scans.
